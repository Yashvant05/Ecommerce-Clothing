About Us

At MenTrend, we're not just a clothing brand — we're a lifestyle movement. Our mission is to redefine modern menswear by combining street-style edge with everyday comfort and affordability.

Founded by fashion-forward thinkers, MenTrend was born out of a desire to bring fresh, bold, and functional fashion to men who value individuality. We design for the trailblazers, the dreamers, the creators — for the men who walk with confidence and wear their ambition.

From urban essentials to standout statement pieces, each MenTrend item is crafted with attention to detail and quality. We source premium fabrics, follow ethical production practices, and stay ahead of style trends to keep your wardrobe elevated — all year round.

We are proud to be more than a brand. We're a growing community of trendsetters who believe in fashion with attitude, purpose, and style.

Join the MenTrend movement — look sharp, stay bold, be unforgettable.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the shipping charges?

We offer free shipping on all orders above ₹499. For orders below that, a flat shipping charge of ₹49 applies.

How long does delivery take?

Orders are usually delivered within 3-7 business days depending on your location.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 7-day hassle-free return and exchange policy. Items must be unused and in original packaging.

Do you offer size exchanges?

Yes! If the size doesn't fit, you can request a size exchange within 7 days of receiving your order.

How do I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, we'll send you a tracking link via email and SMS.

Is COD (Cash on Delivery) available?

Yes, Cash on Delivery is available on all orders across India with no extra charges.
